Sweet Sunrise

Orange juice and grenadine make a drink as pretty as it is tasty.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 4 oz. orange juice
  • 0.5 oz. grenadine
  • Garnish: orange slice
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Pour orange juice into a glass filled with ice.
  2. Slowly pour the grenadine into the juice, it should sink to the bottom.
  3. Don't stir.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice.

Notes

  • Fresh orange juice is best.
  • Using a high quality or homemade grenadine will really let the flavors shine.
