Virgin Piña Colada

Smooth, refreshing, delcious. Coconut and pineapple bring the beach to you.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 4 oz. pineapple juice
  • 1 oz. cream of coconut
  • 0.5 oz. coconut cream
  • 0.5 cup(s) ice
  • Garnish: maraschino cherry and/or pineapple wedge
  • Preferred drinkware: Hurricane glass

Instructions

  1. Add 1/2 of the ice and rest of ingredients to blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Optional: Add more ice and blend until smooth until you reach your desired consistency.
  4. Garnish with maraschino cherry and/or pineapple wedge.

Notes

  • Chill all ingredients beforehand for best results. You'll get a smoother consistency using less ice that won't water down as much as it melts.
  • You can omit some of the pineapple juice for frozen pineapple for a frothier texture.
