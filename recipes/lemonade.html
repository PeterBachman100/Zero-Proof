Lemonade

Sweet, tart, refreshing. This is summer in a glass.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 6 oz water
  • Garnish: lemon slice
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins Glass

Instructions

  1. Pour the juice and sugar into a glass.
  2. Stir until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Pour in the water and stir.
  4. Add ice if desired.
  5. Garnish with a lemon slice.

Notes

  • Adjust the ratios as desired! Lemons vary in flavor, sweetness, and sourness, so you can always adjust to taste.
  • For making larger batches, the ratio is: 1 part lemon juice, 1 part sugar, 6 parts water.
