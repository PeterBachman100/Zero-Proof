Virgin Old Fashioned

While it is difficult to replicate the complexity of the classic, barley tea and a dash of bitters do a commendable job.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 2 oz. barley tea
  • 1 orange slice(s)
  • 1 sugar cube(s)
  • 2 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Garnish: maraschino cherry & orange slice
  • Preferred drinkware: lowball glass

Instructions

  1. Make the barley tea by steeping one bag in 4 cups of cold water for two hours.
  2. Muddle the sugar, 1 orange slice and bitters in the bottom of the glass.
  3. Add desired amount of ice to glass.
  4. Add the tea and stir until sugar is almost completely dissolved.
  5. Garnish with cherry and orange slice.

Notes

  • Bitters contain trace alcohol, but the amount is so small as to be negligible.
  • Swap the maraschino for a luxardo cherry for an upscale upgrade.
  • Some people prefer lemon instead of orange in this recipe.
  • You can use orange peel instead of the whole orange slice.
