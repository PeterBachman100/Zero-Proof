Honey Basil Lemonade

The basil adds just a touch of complexity to a nice sweet lemonade.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 2 oz. lemon juice
  • 1 oz. honey
  • 0.5 cup(s) loosely packed basil leaves
  • Pinch salt
  • 6 oz. water
  • Garnish: Basil leaves
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Blend lemon juice, honey, basil, and salt on high for 1 minute
  2. Add water and blend until combined.
  3. Strain into a glass filled halfway with ice.
  4. Garnish with a few basil leaves and optional lemon wedge.

Notes

  • As always, using fresh lemon juice will taste best
