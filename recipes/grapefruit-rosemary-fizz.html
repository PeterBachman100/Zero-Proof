Grapefruit & Rosemary Fizz

A little bit of complexity supports the grapefruit in a drink that is well balanced for being so simple.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 4 oz. grapefruit juice
  • 0.5 oz. rosemary simple syrup
  • 6 oz. club soda
  • Garnish: rosemary sprig and optional graperfuit slice.
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass.

Instructions

  1. Add the the juice and rosemary simple syrup to a glass and stir to combine.
  2. Add ice if desired.
  3. Top with club soda.
  4. Garnish with a small rosemary sprig and/or slice of grapefruit.

Notes

  • Play around with the ratios to suit your taste!
