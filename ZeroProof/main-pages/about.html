Welcome to Zero Proof!

Zero Proof is all about mixed drinks that don't contain alcohol.

We have curated recipes for a wide range of drinks, ranging from spruced up sodas like a Shirley Temple, to delicious spins on popular drinks like Blackberry Sage Lemonade, tasty party drinks like a Cinderella and beyond to recreations of cocktails like a Virgin Pina Colada.

Check out the Home page to see the latest recipes, seasonal selections, and updates.


To see all our recipes check out the Recipes page.


To learn more about ingredients, and how to make things like simple syrup, grenadine, or shrubs, look to the Ingredients page.
