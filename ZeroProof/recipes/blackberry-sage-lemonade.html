Blackberry Sage Lemonade

Blackberry brings out a sweeter side of sage you'll be happy to meet.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 1 cup(s) blackberries
  • 2 oz. lemon juice
  • 1 oz. sugar
  • 2 leaves fresh sage
  • Pinch salt
  • 6 oz. water
  • Garnish: Sage and blackberries
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Blend blackberries, lemon juice, sugar, and salt until smooth (about 1 minute)
  2. Lightly muddle sage leaves in the bottom of a glass
  3. Strain the juice mixture into the glass and stir to combine.
  4. Fill the glass the rest of the way with ice.
  5. Garnish with a few blackberries and 1-2 sage leaves that have been clapped between your hands (or otherwise lightly distressed).

Notes

  • Clapping the sage leaf garnish before adding it helps to release the scent.
