Roy Rogers

Cola gets brightened with a splash of grenadine for an easy upgrade.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 oz. grenadine
  • 8 oz. cola
  • Garnish: maraschino cherry
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Pour the grenadine into a glass filled with ice.
  2. Top with cola.
  3. Stir well.
  4. Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

Notes

  • If available, use a cola with less sweetness; colas from less recognized brands may bring unique flavors to enjoy.
  • Swap the maraschino cherry for a luxardo cherry for an upscaled flavor.
  • Adding splashes of different flavored sodas or simple syrups can add plenty of fun and interesting twists to this drink.
