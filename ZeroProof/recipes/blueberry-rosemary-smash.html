Blueberry & Rosemary Smash

Delicious blueberry balanced with a bit of complexity from rosemary.

Ingredients

  • Leaves from a small rosemary sprig
  • 7-8 blueberries
  • 1 oz. honey syrup
  • 1 oz. lemon juice
  • 4 oz. club soda
  • Garnish: rosemary sprig & blueberries
  • Preferred drinkware: lowball

Instructions

  1. Muddle the rosemary leaves, blueberries, and honey syrup in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add lemon juice to cocktail shaker and fill halfway with ice.
  3. Shake and strain into a glass.
  4. Top with club soda.
  5. Garnish with skewered blueberries and rosemary sprig.

Notes

  • Use fresh lemon juice.
  • You could use frozen blueberries if that's all that is available.
  • You can swap the club soda for seltzer or sparkling mineral water.
