Strawberry Jalapeno Lemonade

Sweet, hot, and refreshing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of hulled and quartered strawberries
  • 3 thin slices of jalapeno, seeds and ribs removed
  • 2 oz. lemon juice
  • 1.5 oz. sugar
  • 6 oz. water
  • Pinch salt
  • Garnish: Strawberry and jalapeno slice
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Combine strawberries, jalapeno, sugar, and salt in a bowl and toss to coat. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove jalapeno slices and a few strawberry pieces to use a the garnish.
  3. Blend the rest of the strawberries with lemon juice on high speed until smooth (about 1 minute).
  4. Add cold water and blend briefly to mix.
  5. Strain the mixture into a glass.
  6. Put the strawberries you pulled out earlier into the glass.
  7. Garnish with a strawberry and a slice of jalapeno.

Notes

  • Macerating the strawberries and jalapenos can seem like a bit of work, but is well worth the effort!
  • If you want to swap the lemon juice for lime, you could make this a version of a margarita!
