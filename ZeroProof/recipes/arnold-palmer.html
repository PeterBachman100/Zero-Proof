Arnold Palmer

A simple and refreshing mix of iced tea and lemonade makes for easy summertime sipping.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 3 oz. lemonade
  • 6 oz. iced tea
  • Garnish: lemon wedge
  • Preferred drinkware: tall glass

Instructions

  1. Pour lemonade and iced tea into a glass with ice.
  2. Stir.
  3. Garnish with lemon wedge.

Notes

  • Use black tea for the iced tea, oolong is recommended.
  • Make fresh homemade lemonade to bring this drink up a notch.
