Shirley Temple

An American classic. Fizzy, sweet, and bright. This drink is good simple fun.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz. lemon-lime soda
  • 3 oz. ginger ale
  • 1/4 oz. grenadine
  • Garnish: maraschino cherry
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Pour grenadine into glass with ice.
  2. Add equal parts lemon-lime soda and ginger ale.
  3. Stir.
  4. Garnish with maraschino cherries.

Notes

  • Experiment with the brands and ratios of sodas to find what you like best!
  • Sub the ginger ale for ginger beer to add some complexity and bite.
