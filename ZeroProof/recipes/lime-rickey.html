Lime Rickey

A simple refreshment with pleasingly sharp tartness.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. lime juice
  • 1 oz. simple syrup
  • 3 oz. seltzer water
  • Garnish: lime wedge
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Stir together the lime juice and simple syrup.
  2. Pour the lime mixture into a glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with seltzer water and stir.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Notes

  • If you want the drink to be less acidic without losing lime flavor use soda water instead of seltzer.
  • Simple drinks let the ingredients shine, so use the freshest soda and limes you can.
  • Try other sodas! Lemon-lime, tonic, ginger-ale, etc.
