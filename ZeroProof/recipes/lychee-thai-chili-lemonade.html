Lychee Thai-chili Lemonade

The floral sweetness of Lychee gets perfectly balanced with heat and tartness.

Ingredients

  • Serves 4-6
  • 1.5 cups of lychee flesh
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • 1/2 small red Thai Chili
  • 3 cups water
  • Garnish: Optional whole Chili
  • Preferred drinkware: Martini Glass

Instructions

  1. Blend lychee, lemon juice, sugar, salt, and Thai chili at high speed until smooth (about 1 minute).
  2. Strain into a pitcher.
  3. Stir in the water and combine thoroughly.
  4. Pour into glasses.
  5. Garnish with optional whole Chili.

Notes

  • Feel free to play with the ratios of lemon juice and Chili if you want it sweeter or hotter.
