Lychee Thai-chili Lemonade

The floral sweetness of Lychee gets perfectly balanced with heat and tartness.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 0.25 cup(s) of lychee flesh
  • 2 oz. lemon juice
  • 1.5 oz. sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • 0.125 small red Thai Chili
  • 6 oz. water
  • Garnish: Optional whole Chili and lychee flesh
  • Preferred drinkware: collins glass

Instructions

  1. Blend lychee, lemon juice, sugar, salt, and Thai chili at high speed until smooth (about 1 minute).
  2. Add water and blend until combined.
  3. Strain into a glass filled halfway with ice.
  4. Garnish with lychee fruit and optional whole Chili.

Notes

  • Feel free to play with the ratios of lemon juice and Chili if you want it sweeter or hotter.
