Raspberry Fizz

A fun and refreshing combination of raspberry and grapefruit.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 2 oz. Raspberry Syrup
  • 1 oz. Red Grapefruit juice
  • 1 cup(s) club soda
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass, or Champagne glass

Instructions

  1. Stir the syrup and juice together.
  2. Pour into a glass filled halfway with crushed ice.
  3. Top with club soda.

Notes

  • You can get raspberry syrup from the store in the coffee aisle. Better yet, make fresh homemade raspberry syrup.
