Virgin Moscow Mule

Ginger beer and lime strike a beautiful, fizzy balance.

Ingredients

  • 0.75 oz. lime juice
  • 0.5 cups ginger beer
  • Garnish: Lime wedge and mint
  • Preferred drinkware: copper mug

Instructions

  1. Fill a mug with ice.
  2. Add the lime juice and ginger beer.
  3. Stir.
  4. Garnish with lime wedge and mint sprig.

Notes

  • Fresh lime juice is best.
  • Feel free to play around with the ratios a bit, or even add some simple syrup for sweetness.
