Peach Thyme Lemonade

This is a frothier and richer lemonade, with succulent peach flavor accompanied by the scent of thyme.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 1 fresh peach(es), cut into small pieces
  • 2 oz. lemon juice
  • 1.5 oz. sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp. thyme
  • 6 oz. water
  • Garnish: Thyme sprig and optional peach slice
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Blend peaches, lemon juice, sugar, and salt at high speed until smooth (about 1 minute).
  2. Add water and blend until combined.
  3. Muddle thyme in the bottom of the glass.
  4. Strain peach puree into the glass.
  5. Stir thoroughly to combine.
  6. Add ice if desired.
  7. Garnish with a thyme sprig and optional peach slice.

Notes

  • If fresh peaches are unavailable, frozen is better than canned.
