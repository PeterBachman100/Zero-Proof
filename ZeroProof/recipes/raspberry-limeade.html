Raspberry Limeade

Bright, tart, sweet, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 1 cup(s) raspberries
  • 2 oz. lime juice
  • 1 oz. sugar
  • 6 oz. water
  • Pinch of salt
  • Garnish: raspberries and lime wedge
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Blend the raspberries, lime juice, sugar, and salt on high speed until smooth (about 1 minute).
  2. Add water and blend until combined.
  3. Strain through a fine mesh strainer into a glass filled halfway with ice.
  4. Garnish with raspberries and a lime wedge.

Notes

  • Play with the sweetness level by adjusting the amount of sugar, and add water/ice if the flavor is too strong.
  • Using fresh raspberries is best, but frozen will do a commendable job as well!
