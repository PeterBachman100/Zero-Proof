Virgin Cucumber Gimlet

Refreshing and fizzy.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 1.5 oz. club soda
  • 4 slices cucumber
  • 1 oz. lime juice
  • 1 oz. simple syrup
  • Garnish: cucmber slice
  • Preferred drinkware: lowball glass

Instructions

  1. Muddle the cucumber, lime juice and simple syrup.
  2. Add crushed ice.
  3. Top with club soda and stir.
  4. Garnish with a rolled cucumber slice.

Notes

  • Use fresh lime juice for best results.
  • Club soda helps to balance out the acid of the lime, but if you want more acid you can try a seltzer water instead.
