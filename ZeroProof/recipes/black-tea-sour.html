Black Tea Sour

Potentially the classiest sweet tea you'll ever have.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. brewed black tea, chilled
  • 0.75 oz. lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz. grenadine
  • 0.25 oz. simple syrup
  • 1 egg white
  • Preferred drinkware: cocktail glass

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to cocktail shaker and shake thoroughly.
  2. Add ice to shaker and shake again.
  3. Strain into a chilled glass

Notes

  • Feel free to experiment with black teas, but stray away from anything too fruity or spicy.
  • Fresh is always best, consider even making your own grenadine.
  • Make a chai version by using chai tea and swapping the lemon juice for orange juice.
