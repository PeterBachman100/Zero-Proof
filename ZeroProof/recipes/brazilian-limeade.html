Brazilian Limeade

With the addition of condensed milk, think of this as a more indulgent version of limeade.

Ingredients

Servings: 1
  • 1 oz. lime juice
  • 1.5 oz. sweetened condensed milk
  • pinch salt
  • 6 oz. water
  • Garnish: Lime slice
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients until frothy, about 30 seconds.
  2. Fill glass about half full with ice
  3. Pour blended mix into glass
  4. Garnish with a slice of lime.

Notes

  • Substituting lemon juice for the lime makes an equally delicious lemonade.
