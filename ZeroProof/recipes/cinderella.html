Cinderella

Tropical, refreshing, and with a little bit of complexity.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. lemon juice
  • 1 oz. orange juice
  • 1 oz. pineapple juice
  • Dash of grenadine
  • 2 oz. ginger ale
  • Optional: 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Garnish: pineapple and orange slices
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Pour the juices, grenadine, and bitters into a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well.
  3. Strain into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with ginger ale.
  5. Garnish with pineapple and/or orange slices.

Notes

  • Using the freshest juices possible will really amp up the flavor.
  • The bitters add some complexity to the drink, feel free to try more or fewer dashes.
