Honey Basil Lemonade

The basil adds just a touch of complexity to a nice sweet lemonade.

Ingredients

  • Makes 4 servings
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 2 cups loosely packed basil leaves
  • Pinch salt
  • 3 cups water
  • Garnish: Basil leaves
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Blend lemon juice, honey, basil, and salt on high for 1 minute
  2. Strain into a pitcher
  3. Add water to pitcher and stir thoroughly to combine.
  4. Pour into glasses filled halfway with ice.
  5. Garnish with a few basil leaves and optional lemon wedge.

Notes

  • As always, using fresh lemon juice will taste best
