Watermelon Mint Lemonade

Light and refreshing, mint and watermelon take the lead in this one.

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts 1-inch seedless watermelon chunks
  • 1 cup fresh juice from 10 to 12 lemons
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • pinch salt
  • Cold water as needed
  • 1 small bunch Mint
  • 1 quart ice
  • Garnish: Mint and watermelon
  • Preferred drinkware: Collins glass

Instructions

  1. Blend watermelon, lemon juice, sugar, and salt until smooth (about 1 minute)
  2. Strain mixture into a 1 quart measuring cup
  3. Add water to equal 1 quart
  4. Muddle mint in the bottom of a pitcher
  5. Pour in the watermelon mixture and stir
  6. Fill glasses half way with ice and pour in the drink
  7. Garnish with a mint sprig and a small watermelon slice

Notes

  • Be careful not to muddle the mint too much or the flavor will become bitter.
  • If your watermelon isn't very flavorful, reduce the amount of all the other ingredients.
